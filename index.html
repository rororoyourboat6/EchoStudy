My Story

I struggle with studying. I get distracted easily and sometimes struggle to keep track of what I need to do, how much time I need, and how much time I've already spent. I've tried using apps like Notion, phone notes, sticky notes, Notepad, and Google Sheets. Nothing has worked for me. Nothing feels like it was made with students in mind.

That's why I created EchoStudy - a tool specifically designed for students who want to improve their study habits and academic performance.

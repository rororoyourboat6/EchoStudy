Study smarter, not harder. Use EchoStudy.

Vision:
Provide academically-inclined students with the tools to manage their time, enhance their focus, and achieve their goals, so they are able to be productive and successful.

My Story:
I struggle with studying. I get distracted easily and I sometimes struggle to keep track with what all I need to do, how much time I need for it, and how much time I have already spent. I’ve tried using apps like Notion, the notes app on my phone, sticky notes (the physical ones stuck around my room and the one built-in on Windows), Notepad, Google Sheets. Nothing has worked for me. Nothing feels like it was made for students in mind.

Screenshot of website

Features

  • Daily Study Log: Track the subjects and tasks you are completing each day in a clean, simple format.
  • Built-in Focus Timer: Record exactly how long you spend on each subject
  • Mini Reflections: Quickly reflect on how you felt about the session: focused? distracted? why?
  • Focus Insights: See what time of day you work best and which subjects you work on the most.
  • Weekly Recaps: Just like “Spotify Wrapped,” get a weekly breakdown of trends: your best study day, most-focused subject, and number of tasks completed.
  • Procrastination Detection: Spot tasks that keep getting delayed.
